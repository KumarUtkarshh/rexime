# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone.  
All participants, contributors, and maintainers of this project must abide by this Code of Conduct.

We expect cooperation from all members to help ensure a safe and respectful community.

---

## Standards of Behavior

### Examples of Positive Behavior

- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Showing empathy and kindness towards others.
- Taking responsibility and apologizing when necessary.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

### Examples of Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory remarks based on race, gender, identity, ethnicity, religion, age, disability, or sexual orientation.
- Threatening, violent, or aggressive language.
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without permission.
- Any form of fraud, plagiarism, or intellectual dishonesty.

---

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying acceptable behavior and are empowered to take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

They have the right to:

- Remove or edit comments, commits, or contributions.
- Reject contributions not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
- Temporarily or permanently ban any contributor violating these rules.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness violations of this Code of Conduct, please report privately via:

**Email:** contacts.bitshiftz@gmail.com

We will review and respond within a reasonable timeframe.  
All reports are kept confidential.

---

## Enforcement

Actions may include:

- A private warning.
- Request for corrective action.
- Temporary or permanent ban from the project.

The severity of the response depends on the violation.
